Due to COVID-19 restrictions, we are currently offering contactless delivery and curbside pickup only.

Delivery Schedule

Deliveries occur every Tuesday. Orders must be placed between 8 am and 5 pm on Mondays in order to be delivered that week. We deliver within a 50 mile radius of our kitchen located at 1234 Madison Street in Woodstock, IL.

We offer three options for delivery cadence:

  • Weekly
  • Bi-weekly
  • Monthly

We also offer curbside pickup.

You can build a custom plan based on the number of people in your household and how many days per week you'd like to receive meals for. Please visit our pricing page for plan pricing information.
